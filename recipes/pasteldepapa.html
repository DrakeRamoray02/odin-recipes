Pastel de papa

Pastel de papas is a typical dish of Argentine cuisine, which consists of a type of savory cake, made with layers of mashed potatoes and minced meat.

The meat is usually pre-cooked with onion, garlic and other seasonings, and mixed with beaten egg and grated cheese.

A layer of mashed potatoes is then placed on top of the meat mixture and baked until the top is golden brown and crispy.

    Ingredients
  • 1.5kg of potatoes
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of cream
  • 2 big spoon of butter
  • 1 little spoon of salt
  • 1 spoon of oil
  • 1/2kg of minced meat
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 onion
  • 1 meat broth
  • 1 little spoon of oregano
  • 1/2 little spoon of cumin
  • 1/2 spoon of dust chilli pepper
  • 1/2 cup of olives
  • 3 hard boiled eggs
    Steps
  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into large pieces
  2. Cook them in hot, salted water for 15 minutes or until they are soft.
  3. In a frying pan, heat the oil. Add the meat and cook until browned.
  4. Add the onion along with the garlic, the contents of the Gourmet powdered meat broth packet, and cook until the onion is cooked and the mixture is juicy.
  5. Add Oregano, ground cumin and chili, cook for 1 minute.
  6. Remove the potatoes from the water and grind and add the warm milk, cream, butter and salt; until you obtain a creamy puree
  7. In a dish that can go in the oven, put 1/3 of the mashed potatoes.
  8. Add the meat mixture, distributing it well throughout the pan. Add the olives and hard-boiled egg.
  9. Cover with the rest of the puree.
  10. Place in the oven at medium-high temperature for 15 minutes or until hot and golden.
  11. NOTE: You can put granulated sugar or parmesan cheese on the topping
  12. ENJOY
